Why this guide

Built for your rig,
not just any camper.

Most camping guides were written for tent campers. We built this one specifically for RV travelers — every campground is rated by max rig length, maneuverability, hookup type, and generator policy. No vague descriptions, no outdated info.

Every data point is verified directly from the park, Recreation.gov, or a first-hand visit. If we couldn't verify it, we don't publish it.

📏
Rig-specific data Max length, maneuverability rating, site surface, and pull-through availability for every camp.
⏱️
Drive time from Seattle Real-world drive times, not map distances. Sorted into five ranges so you can plan for your weekend.
🔌
Hookup clarity Full hookups, electric-only, water & electric, or dry camping — always clearly labeled, never guessed.
📡
Cell coverage rating Know before you go whether you'll have signal. Three-tier rating based on on-the-ground reports.
